Where Compliance Goes Wrong

📋

Wrong Rate Applied

Royalty and DMF rates are revised by notification every few years. Apply last year's rate to this year's dispatch — or a current rate to a back-period — and you invite recovery notices, interest, and an inspector's scrutiny.

⏰

Missed Deadlines

Six-monthly environmental compliance reports, annual returns, DMF contributions — each runs on its own calendar. A single lapsed deadline can stall dispatch permissions and freeze a working mine.

💸

Unexpected Penalties

NPV and dead-rent computations for lease grant and renewal are unforgiving. A single error in the calculation can swing the figure by ₹10 lakh or more — and the department's number is the one that counts.
