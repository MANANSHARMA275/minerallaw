EC Compliance Checklist

Step-by-step guide for Rajasthan minor mineral mines. Print this page and carry it to the government office.

Phase 1

EC Application (Environment Clearance)

  • • Prepare Form-1 with mine location, mineral type, lease area
  • • Submit to SEIAA Rajasthan (State Expert Appraisal Committee)
  • • Attach: land documents, NOC from revenue dept, site plan
  • ⚠️ Father verifies: current SEIAA submission address and procedure
Phase 2

Mining Plan Approval

  • • Prepare mining plan per MCDR 2017
  • • Submit to IBM (Indian Bureau of Mines) for minor mineral approval
  • • Attach: EC copy, lease deed, survey maps
  • ⚠️ Father verifies: current IBM office contacts for Rajasthan
Phase 3

Royalty Registration

  • • Register with DMG Rajasthan (Dept of Mines and Geology)
  • • Obtain royalty passbook
  • • First payment: within 30 days of production start
  • ⚠️ Father verifies: exact DMG registration procedure 2026
Phase 4

DMF Registration

  • • Register with District Mineral Foundation
  • • DMF rate: 10% of royalty for minor minerals (⚠️ Father confirms)
  • • Payment: monthly, with royalty payment
  • • Maintain payment receipts for 7 years
Phase 5

6-Monthly EC Compliance Report

  • • Submit to SEIAA within 30 days of each 6-month period
  • • Include: production data, environmental monitoring, water usage
  • • Attach: photographs of site and plantation work
  • ⚠️ Father verifies: exact due dates and submission format
Phase 6

Annual Return (DMG)

  • • Submit annual return to DMG Rajasthan by 31st March each year
  • • Include: annual production, royalty paid, employment data
  • ⚠️ Father verifies: exact form name and submission portal

⚠️ This checklist is for general guidance only. Every mine's EC conditions are different. Verify each step against your actual EC approval letter. Use Ask Expert for mine-specific guidance.
